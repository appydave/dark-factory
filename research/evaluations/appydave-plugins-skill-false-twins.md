## Notes
The "crossover point" verdict structure (what would you have to believe for each choice to be correct?) is more epistemically honest than feature-checklist comparisons and more useful for decision-making under uncertainty. The five-source triangulation rule (README, issue tracker, maintainer talk, bidirectional migration, own benchmark) is a practical research protocol. Top 3 quality candidate alongside `doubt-driven-development` and `test-driven-development`.
